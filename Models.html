Models of Deafness

Medical

Deafness is treated as a physical condition that needs be treated. The degrees of hearing end up defining the person.

Terms: hearing impaired, hearing loss, abnormal hearing,

Cultural

Deaf people are able to regard themselves and their interpretatiof the world as the norm. Deafness is a culture bound by shared languages and experiences with deafness.

Preferred Terms: Deaf, hard of hearing, hearing

Terms: hearing impaired implies disability and is considered a slur

Social

Deaf people are only disabled if there is not a way to access language. Hearing people failing to provide accessibility (intentionally or not) to the deaf community creates exclusion. A minority group will have a difficult time getting acesss without support from the majority.

Terms: deaf, Deaf, hard of hearing, hearing loss

Educational

Deafness and Hearing Impaired are categories of services in public school services. Your ability to receive educational services depends on how assessments evaluate your deficits. You are classified by the impact of your hearing loss on educational performance.

Terms: Deafness, Hearing Impairment
